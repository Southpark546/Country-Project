Tacos

Tacos are a staple of Mexican cuisine, featuring various fillings in soft or crispy tortillas.

Guacamole

Guacamole is a popular avocado-based dip or spread originating from Mexico.

Mole

Mole is a complex sauce, often containing chocolate, that's a hallmark of Mexican cooking.

Tamales

Tamales are a traditional Mesoamerican dish made of masa steamed in corn husks or banana leaves.

Pozole

Pozole is a traditional soup or stew made with hominy and meat, typically pork.

Chiles en Nogada

Chiles en Nogada is a poblano chili filled with fruit and meat, covered in a walnut cream sauce.
