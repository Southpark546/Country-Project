History

Mexico is home to ancient civilizations like the Maya and Aztec, with impressive structures like Chichen Itza.

Biodiversity

Mexico is one of the world's most biodiverse countries, hosting 10-12% of global biodiversity.

Food

Mexican cuisine is known worldwide, with dishes like tacos, guacamole, and mole recognized globally.

Day of the Dead

The colorful Day of the Dead festival honors deceased loved ones and is recognized by UNESCO.
